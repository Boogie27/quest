What is the brutal truth about Developers?

Simply put, a camera is a device for recording visual images in the form of photographs, or video signals using either film or digital memory. It is a device that consists of a lightproof chamber with an aperture fitted with a lens and a shutter through which the image of an object is projected onto a surface for recording (as on film) or for translation into electrical impulses (as for television broadcast). The word camera is short for camera obscura, which in Latin means “dark room.” A camera obscura is a darkened enclosure which can be as small as a box or as large as a room into which light is admitted through a very small hole. Because of the way in which light beams cross rather than scatter, an upside-down image of whatever is outside the enclosure is projected on the surface opposite the hole. The image can be made brighter by passing the light through a lens. The first photographic camera was simply a camera obscura with the image projected on light-sensitive chemicals.
